About Pixel Portal

Pixel Portal is a student-made game site built for fun and to learn how websites work. It lets you play games such as Scratch games and others instantly using your browser.

This site was created to explore web development, game design, and how online platforms work — all using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Pixel Portal does not require accounts, downloads, or personal information. Just click a game and play.

Made by a student who loves coding, game design, and building cool things.
